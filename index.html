Rules

  1. Each team starts with 0 drinks at the beginning of the season
  2. If a player scores 0 points in a week, their team owes drinks for those players
  3. If a team fails to complete any owned drinks before the following week kicks off, their drink count doubles the following week
  4. If you owe >= 10 drinks you lose a 1st round pick.
